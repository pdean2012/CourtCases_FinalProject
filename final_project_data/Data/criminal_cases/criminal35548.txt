  (Montréal), Number 500-10-005141-120, 2013 QCCA 1305, dated
  July 31, 2013, was heard on April 23, 2015, and the Court on that
  day delivered the following judgment orally:  [translation] The Chief Justice — We are all of the view that the appeal should be allowed. The
  appeal is allowed and the decision of the Minister of Justice is reinstated
  with reasons to follow. J.C.C. C.J.C.
